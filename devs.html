Developers

CryptoX is a collaborative by the co-creator's Gautam Jha and Jayesh Jain. The main motive behind the app is to bring all the updates from the cryptocurrency market ranging from news to price updates of thousand's of coins and tokens to a single destination where one could easily stay updated with the constantly evolving space. We started working on the app in June'21 and overcame many hurdle's which came along the way. We will be actively contributing to the app after it's release as per the planned roadmap and keep it growing. We intend to launch it on the AppStore soon.
